Binge With Leaf

Leaf is a homegrown headphones brand and we build headphones for those who love to binge watch Movies and TV Shows. Our products are super comfortable for long usage; come with extra long battery life and deliver an enhanced sound with our patented Leaf App.


What makes Leaf different?

While all other headphone brands focus on only music, we focus our efforts to deliver an immersive binge viewing experience to our users. Apart from ensuring comfort and long battery life, we also have Leaf App (patented technology) that enhances the sound of your Leaf Headphones by personalising the audio for you.


Wireless Headphones

Leaf Wireless Headphones are our signature products for those who love to binge watch movies and tv shows. Deep bass, soft leather cushions and extra long battery life make these over ear headphones and on ear headphones a perfect buy to watch video content and listen to music too.


Wireless Earphones

Leaf Wireless Earphones are neckband earphones that are super stylish, lightweight and promise to help you through the day. Ideal for a workplace, bluetooth earphones are one of the top choices of our customers especially for those who need great wireless earphones with mic for calling.


True Wireless Earbuds

Leaf True Wireless Earbuds promise to make you feel free like never before. Powered by the latest bluetooth technology, our Truly Wireless Earbuds offer an unbreakable connection and a seamless experience during usage. The charging case adds to the battery life so you don't have to charge again and again
